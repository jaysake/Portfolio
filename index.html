Meet Baiju Patil

Baiju Patil is an internationally acclaimed wildlife photographer hailing from Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (formerly Aurangabad), Maharashtra, India. Driven by a deep passion for nature and an artistic eye for lighting, his work captures intimate, storytelling moments in the animal kingdom.

For over two decades, Baiju has traversed dense forests, arid deserts, and deep marine environments to bring the beauty and vulnerability of our planet's wildlife into the spotlight. He is an advocate for conservation, using his lens to highlight the urgent need for habitat protection.

Acclaimed Honors

Recipient of major national and state level photography and conservation awards.

Conservation Visualist

Visual contributor to multiple wildlife sanctuaries, publications, and conservation NGOs.
